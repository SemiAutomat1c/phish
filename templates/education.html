What Happened?

This was a simulated phishing attempt. In a real attack, your credentials would now be in the hands of attackers.

Red Flags You Should Have Noticed:

  • The URL in your address bar wasn't the official Google domain
  • There was no HTTPS security indicator
  • The page may have had subtle differences from the real login page

How to Protect Yourself:

  • Always check the URL before entering credentials
  • Look for HTTPS and the lock icon in your browser
  • Enable two-factor authentication when possible
  • Use a password manager to avoid manually typing passwords
